About me

I am currently a PostDoc researcher working with Karen Veroy-Grepl within the Computational Science team.

In my PostDoc I aim to integrate Model Order Reduction technics in a Data Assimilation context (filters, smoother).

During 2019-2022 I was PhD. student under the supervision of Stéphanie Salmon in the team Modélisation Stochastique et Numérique.

My thesis concerns numerical models of fluid flow in the cerebral compartment. It was funded by the ANR project HANUMAN 18-CE45-0014-01. In this work, I tried to use real data acquired at the Amiens Hospital to improve numerical simulations.

Research interest

  • Numerical analysis
  • Finite element method
  • Model order reduction
  • Data assimilation
