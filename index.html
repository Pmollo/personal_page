About me

I am currently a PhD. student under the supervision of Stéphanie Salmon within the team Modélisation Stochastique et Numérique.

My thesis concerns numerical models of fluid flows in biological media and more specifically in the cerebral compartiment. It is funded by the ANR project HANUMAN 18-CE45-0014-01. In my work, I try to use real data acquired at the Amiens CHU to improve numerical simulations. This combines reduced order modelling, numerical analysis and data assimilation.

Research interest

  • Numerical analysis
  • Finite element method
  • Model order reduction
  • Data assimilation
